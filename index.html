About

HackRPI X is the 10th annual intercollegiate hackathon hosted by RPI for students not only from RPI but also from all around the world. Get swag and free food as you compete for exciting prizes! With a broad range of workshops and mentors on-site, thereâ€™s no experience necessary to attend.
